Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy First: Yacht Watch does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Collect

None. Yacht Watch does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from users.

Third-Party Services

Yacht Watch uses the following third-party service:

  • AIS Stream API: We receive real-time vessel tracking data from AIS Stream to display yacht positions. This is a one-way data stream - we only receive yacht position data and do not transmit any user information.

Data Storage

No user data is stored on our servers. The app only temporarily caches yacht position data on your device for display purposes.

Analytics and Tracking

Yacht Watch does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not track your app usage, location, or behavior in any way.

Subscriptions

App Store subscriptions are processed and managed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information or personal details. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles subscription data.

Children's Privacy

Yacht Watch does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages (4+) and does not require any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
